Div alignments

there are several div alignments.

    they are:
  • div align=center>Center-moves text to the middle of the div
  • div align=center>Right-moves text to the right of the div
  • div align=center>Left-moves text to the left of the div
  • div align=justified>justified-spreads text evenly across the entire row
